The Backbone of Corporate Security

On-Site Security Guards

The foundation of corporate security is the on-site security guarding services that provide constant visible presence and deterrence, maintenance of order, and safety of people and property. The following are the roles and responsibilities of on-site security guard services:

Access Control

At the entrance and exit doors in the facility, guards are permanently posted to check the identity of the personnel and allow only cleared staff in the facility.

Patrols and Surveillance

Security personnel are always on the move throughout the compound of your business both within and outside buildings. Hampshire also has security checks again for something strange, or fire violations, or security violations.

Loss Prevention

Preventing theft and vandalism, they distinguish suspicious actions and prevent incidents from happening in the first place.

Emergency Response

Stationary security personnel are easily capable of providing quick reactions to occurrences of an emergency.

A Flexible Security Solution

Mobile Patrol Services

Mobile patrol services have wide coverage compared to onsite guards and are thus ideal for businesses with multiple locations and large properties or those that need off-hour checks. How mobile patrols work: Guards in marked/unmarked vehicles carry out scheduled &/or unscheduled rounds within your premises.

Perimeter Checks

They patrol around the compound scanning for any signs of breakage, destruction, or any other oddities that may exist in the house.

Building Checks

Sometimes they can get and scan inside buildings for signs of intrusion, fire danger, or abandoned riches.

Locking and Unlockingn

Security guards can be relied on to secure your premises by often locking and unlocking your facilities at the right time.

Alarm Response

Mobile patrols can be used to respond to triggered security alarms as and when they happen, this will ensure faster response and possibly reduce the occurrences of the crime.

Venue Security

A Crucial Safeguard During Vulnerable Times

Fire Watch Security

Protection against fires is very important in any structure. Nevertheless, it is possible to face circumstances when a fire protection measure of some buildings is temporarily turned off. That is where fire watch security comes in – an imperative service that provides safety to your property and the occupants in case of a fire outbreak if your fire alarms or sprinkler systems are not functional.

Maintenance and Repair

In the case of planned or emergency work on fire alarms, sprinklers or other fire protection equipment, a fire watch might be required to be constant.

Construction Activities

Some construction tasks such as welding or hot works may cause circumstances that are fire-hazardous for a period. Fire watch guards are something very important during such periods to monitor fire risks.

Building Code Violations

In some cases where a building is declared to have violated regulations in so far as fire is concerned due to failure in fire safety equipment, a fire watch security may be required until such a time when the problem is rectified.

Special Events

Certain situations such as circumstances under which there are uses of open flames or pyrotechnics or the conditions under which there are large crowds in buildings that either do not have sufficient sprinkler systems or have been disabled by water shortages may require implementation of fire watch security.
