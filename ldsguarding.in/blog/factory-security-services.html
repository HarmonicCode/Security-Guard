Factory Security Services

When it comes to the modern industrial environment it is crucial to maintain industrial safety and security. Production lines involve costly items of machinery, valuable inputs and outputs hence factories are vulnerable to theft, vandals and other security risks. Understanding these difficulties, LDS Guarding Solutions has been leading among other security services in Delhi and beyond offering outstanding factory security services for more than 25 years.  

Why is Factory Security so Important?

Factories too are likely to be open 24/7 and are susceptible to access and mishaps especially at off production periods. Also, there are some external hazards and internal hazards that threaten the running of factories; these include; accidents, employees’ fraudulent activities and insubordination. A suitable security measure is not only for physical resources, but for a secure and optimal operation of services.  

Why is LDS Guarding Solutions a preferred choice?

Now, a notable security company, LDS Guarding Solutions has been in operation for more than 25 years. Here’s why we are the preferred choice for factory security:  

1. Experienced Personnel:

   Factory environments are complex and our security guards are trained accordingly based on the policy. They are well-placed to control accesses and monitors, surveillance systems and adequately handle events of emergency.  

2. Tailored Security Plans:

   Depending on the type of the factory, its production capacity or location, its security requirements are most likely to be different from those of others. We then make an evaluation of your facility and derive a security plan that will most suit your facility’s security.  

3. Technology Integration:

   LDS Guarding Solutions employs personnel in conjunction with technological services such as closed-circuit television, identification management system, and alarm systems.  

4. 24/7 Vigilance:

   Being fully awake all times of the day and night, our guards guarantee protection to your factory both during and after working hours.  

Key Services we offer

Access Control Management: Restrict or control people's access into your factory compound to curb cases of intruders getting into the compound frequently.

Surveillance Monitoring: Maintain a strict vigil over certain sensitive parts with the help of latest CCTV managed and operated by skilled manpower.

Emergency Response: Quick response to fire incidents, medical problems, or security issues so that the extent of harm is put as low as is possible to mitigate.

Asset Protection: Protection from theft and damage of costly equipment, materials and products.

Visitor Management: Managing visitor convenience while controlling their access, and effectively managing all supplier and contractor interactions.
