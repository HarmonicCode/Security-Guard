Security Guard for Apartment

People occupying apartment buildings are more sensitive to security since many families and persons stay in a facility at the same time. Employment of a full-time security guard increases safety for all tenants and residents, increases property value, and promotes unity of the residents. As threats have grown over the years in the form of theft, acts of vandalism and unauthorized access, hiring of security personnel becomes crucial, especially for diverse apartment units.

Why Security Guards Are Important for an Apartment?

Preventing of Criminal Acts

Police report shows that having a man in a security uniform discourages criminals from attempting acts such as burglaries or trespassing on the premises. It is obvious that no criminal will venture to steal property or anything else if he or she knows that is well guarded.

Control Over Access

Apartments also comprise many access doors and there are always various people coming in and out of the building. Security also regulates access by frequently checking those entering the compound as visitors, suppliers, and delivery boys and allowing only those who have every right to be there.

Safety for Residents

The security guards offer a sense of security to the residents; anybody feels more secure knowing that there is always someone who can respond to calamities or possibly misconduct. This affirmation will lead to improving quality of life.

Emergency Response

Alarming situations such as fire, medical emergencies, and power outages among other disasters are well dealt with by trained security guards. The capacity for them to synchronize their actions with emergency services can make huge differences in as far as lessening losses or injuries is concerned.

Community Building

Security guards are fixed at certain places and get acquainted with the people within that particular area they are guarding. Residents feel more comfortable and willing to work with other people of similar status whenever these professionals are present.

Working Structure of Apartment Security Guards

24/7 Surveillance

Security guards are always present on the premises and either surveillance through patrols or CCTV cameras. Security guards patrol parking lots, stairwells, corridors and other areas which are open to the public with an aim of checking on everything.

Emergency Response

Like in a fire incident or any medical emergency or power failure situation, security guards are well-placed to address any of these with much promptness. Their capacity to communicate with the emergency agencies can help to reduce one or the other impacts or loss.

Community Building

A security guard can come into contact with residents on a regular basis and the residents get used to seeing the guard. They create an atmosphere of rapport and fruitful collaboration with people who live in the area.

Responsibilities of Apartment Security Guards

24/7 Surveillance

There is ample security on the premises both in terms of human security guards who make rounds within the buildings and by close-circuit television. They also patrol around the clock, observing parking lots, stairwells, corridors and general areas for tidiness.

Visitor Management

They sign visitors’ books, ask for identification and provide information on apartments that guests want to access. This process will also give some control over who gets into the property and monitor who comes in.

Incident Reporting

Guards record all occurrences including any suspicious activities, accidents and or breaches and forthwith give the reports to the apartment management or security agencies.

Conflict Resolution

People who live in multi-tenanted units confront conflict with one another from time to time. Security guards play a role of moderation for parties to the disputes and are responsible for bringing order in perceived disarray.

Maintenance Checks

Security guards are equally responsible for making periodic inspections of important facilities such as fire alarms, lights and surveillance cameras; to ascertain if they are in proper working condition.

Advantages of Hiring Professional Security Guards

Enhanced Safety

The employment of skilled security guards, enables the security personnel to handle diverse challenges, making them more professional when compared to non-professionals.

Reduced Liability

When employing professional guards for apartments, the management is able to save itself from incidences of theft or accidents within the compound.

Improved Reputation

When the landlords have put in place standard security measures, more people will be attracted to the apartments which is good for business; Second, the value of the property increases due to high demand by qualified tenants.

How to select the best security service?

Before choosing security guards for an apartment complex some of the considerations that should be made include; regard that is held by the provider, training and certifications of the guards, and the experience of the guest in managing security for residential areas.
